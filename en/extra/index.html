Arrival

For international arrivals, Pavia is easily accessible from Milan. The most convenient airport is Milan Linate, connected by metro line M4 to Milano Dateo station, where you can take the S13 suburban train directly to Pavia. You can also use Milan Malpensa airport, taking the Malpensa Express train to Milan Central Station, and from there take a regional train to Pavia. Milan Bergamo airport is quite far.

Accommodation

For accommodation, we suggest the following options:

  • Hotel Excelsior is located near the Pavia railway station. We suggest this option if you want to take the opportunity to visit the cities of Pavia and Milan. You can book a room with the discount code ME12A24 on the Hotel’s website or by phone.
  • Hotel Flower is located in Gropello near the venue. We suggest this option if you are arriving by car or prefer a cheaper option. You can book a room with the discount code ME12A24 by writing to the email address info@hotelmotelflower.it or by phone. The hotel can offer a pick-up service from the Pavia railway station.

In Pavia, there are also many small, reasonably priced Bed and Breakfasts.

Venue

Cerimony and reception will take place at Tenuta Cortebella, in Gropello Cairoli, 20 minutes by car from da Pavia.

To facilitate reaching the venue, we will organize a shuttle bus for our guests. The meeting point is the Pavia railway station. The shuttle bus will also be available for the return trip, departing from the venue at the end of the party.
