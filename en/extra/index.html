Arrival

For international arrivals, Pavia is easily accessible from Milan. The most convenient airport is Milan Linate, connected by metro line M4 to Milano Dateo station, where you can take the S13 suburban train directly to Pavia. You can also use Milan Malpensa airport, taking the Malpensa Express train to Milan Central Station, and from there take a regional train to Pavia. Milan - Bergamo airport is not recommended as it is quite far.

Venue

The ceremony and reception will take place at Tenuta Cortebella, in Gropello Cairoli, 20 minutes from Pavia.

To facilitate reaching the venue, we will organize a shuttle bus for our guests. The meeting points are the Pavia railway station at 11:15 AM, and at Hotel Flower at 11:45 AM.

The shuttle bus will also be available for the return trip, departing from the venue around 11:00 PM, at the end of the celebration.
